ABOUT ME

Introduction

Name: Chelsea Angelina Mark Age : 20 Occupation: designer Live : London, United Kingdom

Brief Bio

Chelsea is a talented and innovative designer with a passion for luxury fashion and timeless elegance. Chelsea quickly gained recognition for their unique design aesthetic, blending contemporary creativity with classic sophistication.

Privilege Of Working

Over the years, Chelsea had the privilege of working with some of the most prestigious brands in the fashion industry, including a standout role at Chanel. This experience provided a deep understanding of the craftsmanship and innovation that define Chanel, shaping Chelsea's approach to design and solidifying their reputation within the luxury fashion world.

Now

Now, Chelsea continues to create designs that reflect a refined sense of artistry, functionality, and elegance, bringing a fresh perspective to the ever-evolving world of fashion.
